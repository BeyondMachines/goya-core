Beyondmachines just works

Our automated process has you covered. You can focus on other priorities.
Jump in the conversation only if you want to further emphasize a topic.

Gather raw data

There are tens of cybersecurity events happening daily - incidents, vulnerabilities and trends.
We collect them to understand the current state of play and the global risk posture companies are exposed to.

Filter, make sense

We filter and correlate events to identify those which are relevant, and we try to understand the "Why" and "How" of the event.
We select the applicable events worthy of attention and give context and human impact to them.

Simple, clear information

We provide information to you in simple and clear language, easy to understand regardless of technical skill.
We supplement information with clear action items.

Minimal noise

We notify you directly only in four cases - (i) a problem with us providing the service; (ii) an action that you need to take; (iii) a new feature that you can benefit from; (iv) summary report of our work for you
